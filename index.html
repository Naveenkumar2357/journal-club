About the Club

The Heavy-Ion Collision Phenomenology Journal Club meets weekly to discuss relevant topics in heavy-ion collision (HIC) physics, with an emphasis on recent developments and new advances in the field.

Each week, a member presents a paper, followed by a Q&A and a discussion session.
